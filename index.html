Learning Outcomes

What will students learn?

Outcome 01

Engineering Fields

Identifies basic engineering fields (civil, electronics, agroindustrial, environmental) and explains their connection to sustainability.

Outcome 02

Design Process

Applies the engineering design process (Ask, Imagine, Plan, Create, Improve) to a real-world sustainability challenge.

Outcome 03

English Vocabulary

Uses appropriate English vocabulary and structures to describe the functions of objects, materials, and simple machines.

Outcome 04

Environmental & Social Impact

Analyzes the environmental and social impact of engineering solutions: materials, energy, water, and waste.

Outcome 05

Collaborative Final Project

Designs, prototypes, and presents a solution to a local sustainability problem using ICT tools, working as a team.

CLIL Competencies

The 5Cs of the CLIL Approach

Content

Identify and explain key principles from different engineering fields and their role in solving real sustainability problems.

Communication

Use appropriate vocabulary and structures in English to discuss, describe, and present ideas orally and in writing.

Cognition

Analyze and evaluate different solutions considering advantages, limitations, and impact on society and the environment.

Culture

Demonstrate awareness of how engineering projects affect communities and reflect on their social and environmental implications.

Community

Engage with local and global communities to identify real needs, co-create solutions, and take responsibility for the social impact of engineering decisions.

MEN Standards — Integrated Areas

Mathematics

Mathematical concepts, measurements, and calculations to design and evaluate engineering projects.

Language Arts

Produce and interpret texts to communicate ideas in academic and real contexts.

Natural Sciences

Scientific principles (thermodynamics, environment) that explain how engineering systems work.

Social Studies

Analyze the impact of technological developments in social and economic contexts.

Arts Education

Use artistic and visual languages to communicate ideas creatively in project design.
